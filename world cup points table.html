World Cup 2023: India crush Sri Lanka, become first team to qualify for semifinals

Nov 02, 2023, 09:39PM ISTSource: TOI.in In a dominant display, India secured their spot in the World Cup 2023 semifinals by crushing Sri Lanka. India's sensational bowling performance restricted Sri Lanka to just 55 runs. Sri Lanka was chasing a massive 358 target to win in Mumbai, but dismally slumped to 14-6 inside 10 overs.Mohammed Shami's impressive 5-18 figures led to a crushing 302-run victory in 19.4 overs. India had earlier posted a formidable 357-8 total, with notable contributions from Shubman Gill (92), Virat Kohli (88), and Shreyas Iyer (82), establishing their unbeaten status in the tournament.
